# MediPreCheck

> **Let doctors diagnose, not interrogate.**

## Overview

MediPreCheck is a patient pre-consultation platform designed to streamline doctor appointments by collecting essential medical information before the patient arrives for their consultation.

Patients complete a structured questionnaire while booking an appointment, providing details about their symptoms, medical history, medications, allergies, and other relevant health information.

This information is then shared with the doctor before the consultation, reducing repetitive questioning and allowing more time for diagnosis and treatment.

---

## Problem Statement

Doctors often spend a significant portion of consultation time gathering basic information such as:

* What symptoms are you experiencing?
* How long have you had these symptoms?
* Are you currently taking any medications?
* Do you have any allergies?
* Have you experienced similar issues before?

These questions are repeated for nearly every patient, consuming valuable consultation time and sometimes resulting in incomplete or inconsistent information.

---

## Solution

MediPreCheck collects patient information before the appointment through a guided intake questionnaire.

The platform helps:

* Collect symptom details
* Record symptom duration and severity
* Capture medical history
* Store allergy and medication information
* Provide structured patient data to doctors before the consultation

This enables doctors to review patient information in advance and conduct more focused and efficient consultations.
